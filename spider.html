> 🕷 SpideySenses

SpideySenses is a real-time monophonic FM synthesis engine with dynamic feedback shaping and envelope modulation, designed for tactile responsiveness and physical expressivity.

It features:

  • Frequency modulation synthesis with real-time control over modulation index and carrier drift, mimicking strain and tension in elastic materials.
  • A custom ADSR envelope generator with hysteresis-based triggering and envelope decay mapped to external energy loss (e.g., damping in a stretched web).
  • Feedback-induced pitch deviation and nonlinear response shaping, simulating vibrational coupling as seen in stretched surfaces or soft-body interactions.
  • Time-smoothed control parameters (mod index, envelope target, feedback depth) act as continuous sensory inputs, altering the state of the system in a way reminiscent of mechanoreceptive touch sensors.
  • Synthesized output merges a stable drone field with transient pluck excitation, modulated by envelope amplitude and feedback energy.
